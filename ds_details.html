Nintendo DS Features

Cost

The Nintendo DS Lite isn't officially being sold by Nintendo anymore, so cost isn't a definite value.

Color

The Nintendo DS Lite comes in several different colors. These consist of:

  • Onyx Black
  • Gloss Silver
  • Metallic Rose
  • Ice Blue
  • Enamel Navy
  • Crystal White
  • Polar White
  • Crimson Black
  • Coral Pink
  • Cobalt Blue
  • Red
  • Green
  • Turquoise

Size

Outer Dimensions:

  • Length: 5.2 inches
  • Width: 2.9 inches
  • Height: 0.85 inches

Screen Sizes:

  • Top Screen: 3.12 inches with 256x192 pixels of resolution
  • Bottom Screen: Same as top screen, but touch screen

Attachments

The Nintendo DS Lite comes with a set of features that prove to be useful and effective. The consist of:

  • An embedded stylus pen that has a dedicated slot in the DS, which can be used for the touch screen
  • 19 hours of battery life, and 3 hours of charging to full battery
  • Slot for Nintendo DS game cards and Gameboy Advance Game Paks
  • Wi-fi support
  • Touch Screen, integrated microphone for voice recognition, A/B/X/Y button, control pad, L and R Buttons, START and SELECT Buttons
  • Surround sound
  • PictoChat which can hold up to 16 players online
